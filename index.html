A voice for Lyme-like disease in Western Australia.

In excess of 500 cases of Lyme Disease have been diagnosed in Western Australia in the past three years. Despite this, the condition has yet to receive official recognition from the Australian government and medical community. The WA Lyme Association was founded by patients in August 2012 with the intention of providing peer support, information sharing, facilitating relevant research and advocating toward policy change for Lyme Disease.
